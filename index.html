About Us

Who We Are

Wish Youth Network Society (WYNS) is a provincial, student-led, non-profit organization that seeks to provide hope to youths with serious medical conditions as well as their families via community engagement activities. Our mission is to rekindle and sustain hope for those families who are affected, as well as to foster a community of optimism for support. By engaging the community in these activities, we look to empower the community through outreach in the youth and those we support.

What We Do

We reach out to local hospitals to setup events such as christmas gifting and bake/drink sales for these children in need. Hoping to be a positive influence, Wish Youth Network encourages students to volunteer for our events to gain interaction with these kids in a troubling time of their life.

1

Create opportunities for volunteers to visit hospitals, and interact with patients.

2

Raise funds for various research opportunities, namely, cancer research.

3

Help out those who cannot afford needed operations and surgeries by providing funds.
